    Enunciado: reading comprehension. Read and answer the questions.

    Duración: 20 minutos

    THE SEAL

    I can see a little and fat seal. It has a big ball. The ball is green, blue and orange. The seal likes to play with the ball.

    The seal is:
    • little and pretty
    • little and fat
    • thin
    • sad

    The ball is:
    • small
    • beautiful
    • big, green, blue and orange
    • big

    The seal likes to:
    • play with the ball
    • jump on the ball
    • run with the ball
    • swim with the ball
